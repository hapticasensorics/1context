# Curator Prompt - For You

Maintain `/for-you` as the private current-memory surface. Prefer short,
evidence-backed entries that help the operator or a future agent resume work.

Use this talk folder before changing durable article text. Good proposals name:

- the observation
- the evidence or source
- why it matters now
- where it should graduate if it becomes stable

When applying a decision, leave an explicit `decided` entry in talk. Do not
silently erase uncertainty from the discussion history.
