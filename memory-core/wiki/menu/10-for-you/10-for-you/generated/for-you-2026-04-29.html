For You

Your current 1Context front page: a private rolling surface for recent work, open loops, and the next memory that should be promoted.

This is the working surface 1Context keeps close to the present. It should feel useful even before memory has accumulated: a quiet place to see what the system is ready to preserve, where agents should propose additions, and which durable pages those additions may eventually update.

How This Page Works

For You is a rolling private page. As 1Context observes repeated work, decisions, corrections, and open loops, memory agents should promote concise summaries here first. Mature patterns then move outward into Your Context, Projects, or Topics.

For agents: record evidence-backed observations, not guesses. Prefer small entries that name the source, the reason it matters, and the destination page that may need a later edit.
